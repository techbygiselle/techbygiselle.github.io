The Lazy River

If you love floating down the Chippewa River, but you cant wait for the warm weather to return, then the Chaos lazy river is calling YOUR name! The lazy river is extremely relaxing, and makes up almost half of the water park, you will definitely be feeling calm and composed by the end of your float! Oh, and did we mention that theres a DOUBLE hot tub?!

Its Perfect for a Party

Chaos has cute, cozy cabanas, equipped with tables and chairs, where you can host a party. You can reserve one (or all) of the cabanas ahead of time; so, why not go there for your childs next birthday party or to celebrate the end of finals with a few friends? Order a pizza or two for the group, go for a little float or an exhilarating ride and have some fun!
